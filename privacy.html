Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

Overview

开物 Kaiwu does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All features run entirely on your device. We have no servers, no user accounts, and no analytics.

This privacy policy explains our practices regarding information handling for the Kaiwu mobile application ("App"), published by Kaiwu.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Kaiwu does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Health or biometric data
  • Financial information
  • Contacts, photos, or other device content

Local Data Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Divination history — Records of your past divination sessions
  • Favorites — Divination results you choose to save
  • Theme preference — Your selected visual theme
  • Check-in records — Daily check-in and blessing participation history

This data is stored using your device's local storage (SharedPreferences) and is never transmitted to any external server. Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all locally stored data.

Third-Party Services

Kaiwu does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The App does not include:

  • Analytics SDKs (no Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.)
  • Advertising frameworks
  • Social media tracking pixels
  • Crash reporting services that collect user data

Network Usage

All divination calculations are performed locally on your device using built-in algorithms. The App does not require an internet connection to function. No data is sent to or received from external servers during normal use.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. Since no personal data is collected from any user, the App is compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device and is never transmitted, there is no risk of data breach from our end. We recommend keeping your device secured with a passcode or biometric lock to protect your local data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
