应用简介

开物是一款专注于中华传统文化知识学习的教育应用，提供系统化的课程体系：

  • 易经入门 — 太极阴阳、八卦、六十四卦系统讲解
  • 五行学说 — 五行生克、对应关系、哲学应用
  • 天干地支 — 干支纪年、六十甲子、纳音五行
  • 紫微斗数入门 — 十二宫位、十四主星、星曜体系
  • 八字命理基础 — 四柱概念、日主、十神体系
  • 姓名学基础 — 五格剖象法、笔画分析

每节课配有互动测验，帮助检验学习效果。所有数据仅存储在本地设备上。

仅供文化学习参考。

About Kaiwu

Kaiwu is an educational app dedicated to Chinese traditional culture knowledge, offering structured courses:

  • I Ching Fundamentals — Taiji, Yin-Yang theory, Eight Trigrams, 64 Hexagrams
  • Five Elements Theory — Generation/Overcoming cycles, correspondences, philosophy
  • Heavenly Stems & Earthly Branches — Chinese calendar system, 60-year cycle
  • Ziwei Doushu Introduction — 12 palaces, 14 main stars, star system
  • BaZi Fundamentals — Four Pillars, Day Master, Ten Gods system
  • Name Study Basics — Five Grid method, stroke analysis

Each lesson includes interactive quizzes. All data is stored locally on your device.

For cultural learning purposes only.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does the app collect my personal information?
A: No. All features run entirely on your device. No account required, no data uploaded. See our Privacy Policy.
Q: Will my learning progress be lost?
A: Progress is stored locally. Uninstalling the app will clear progress data.
Q: What devices are supported?
A: iPhone and iPad running iOS 15 or later.
Q: Is the content free?
A: Yes, all courses are completely free with no in-app purchases.
Q: How do I switch themes?
A: Go to "Profile" tab to choose from four themes: Clean Light, Warm Ivory, Dark, and Neon Night.
